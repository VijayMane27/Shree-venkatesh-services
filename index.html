About Us

The Leading Construction Services Company

We, Shree Venkatesh Construction Services, situated at Budhgaon, Sangli, Maharashtra, are engaged in supplying a wide range of excavators, earthmoving machinery and construction equipment on hire. From single machines to complete fleets of vehicles, we are expert in supplying reliable affordable machinery. We also provide quality core cutting, drilling and demolition services to our honored patrons. We have expertise for supplying machinery to key industries and can help your organization get moving on infrastructure and mining projects. The focus has always been to deliver reliable and highly productive machinery. We aim to continuously pursue high levels of customer satisfaction and have always been supportive in addressing customer concerns.
